About Rajib Majumdar

A lifelong music lover, guitarist, songwriter and independent artist, beginning a second innings to fulfil a lifelong passion through original music.

Music Beyond Boundaries

After an enriching journey with the Indian Air Force, HCL and Bajaj Electricals, I have begun my second innings to fulfil my lifelong dream of creating meaningful music.

My songs combine heartfelt lyrics, expressive guitar, modern production and timeless melodies that connect people through love, hope and unity.
